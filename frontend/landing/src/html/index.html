About Longevity token

Eterly is the world’s first application and platform designed and built by longevity experts, to promote health and fitness, anti-aging, and personalised health diagnostics.

Our front-facing app integrates seamlessly with fitness trackers including Fitbit and Apple Watch, to collect and store users health and fitness data, and to interact with the user via an AI-driven intelligent chatbot, which acts like a virtual personal trainer.

Behind our app sits a platform, built using the blockchain, which houses the Eterly ecosystem and is fueled by its own token - Longevity Token (LTY).

Users will be able to upload and store their health and fitness data privately, share their data with third parties at their discretion, “mine” LTY tokens by completing fitness and health related tasks, that unlock additional products and services, distributed via a curated Open Marketplace, and interact with other users.

Above all users will gain from personalized health, lifestyle and fitness advice from world-leading experts in the field of gerontology, bio-pharma, personalized medicine and ground-breaking research into longevity, through programs tailored to them using the latest AI and machine learning algorithmic techniques.
